Case: aria-owns

The example on this page uses the aria-owns attribute to establish the parent/child relationship between the two lists.

Example

Fruit
Vegetables
Apples
Bananas

Code


<div role="list">
<div role="listitem" aria-owns="fruit">Fruit</div>
<div role="listitem">Vegetables</div>
</div>

<div role="list" id="fruit">
<div role="listitem">Apples</div>
<div role="listitem">Bananas</div>
</div>

Expected behaviour

The code should cause screen readers to behave as though the two lists are nested (with apples and banas appearing as children of fruit).
